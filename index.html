Coffee Beans

coffee bean is a seed of the coffee plant and the source for coffee beverages. The coffee plant is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree of the genus Coffea.

Coffee Cupping

Coffee cupping is the practice of observing the tastes and aromas of brewed coffee. It is a standard practice in the coffee industry used by professionals to evaluate coffee beans.

Latte Art

Latte art is a method of preparing coffee created by pouring steamed milk into a shot of espresso and resulting in a pattern or design on the surface of the latte.
